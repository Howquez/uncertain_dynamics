How the Study Works

This study runs for multiple periods. What makes each period important is that:

  1. Your earnings from each period become your starting amount ("endowment") for the next period.
  2. In each period, you will decide how to allocate your current tokens between two accounts.
  3. Your endowment for the next period will equal the tokens you earned from both accounts in the current period.
  4. The process repeats until the 10th period, when your total accumulated tokens determine your earnings.

Accounts & Returns

At the beginning of each period, you will allocate your endowment between a private account and a group account.

The tokens that you place in the private account have a return of 1. This means that, at the end of a given period, your private account will contain exactly the amount of tokens you put into the private account at the beginning of the period. Nobody except yourself benefits from your private account.

The tokens you place in the group account are combined with tokens from the other three group members. Each member receives a share of this combined pool. You will always receive a return rate of at least 27.5% of the total tokens in the group account, regardless of how many tokens you personally contributed.

Your exact return rate is individual and can vary from period to period (which is why we will provide that information on your screen). It depends on how your endowment compares to others in your group:

  • If you have a smaller portion of the total endowments in your group, you'll receive a higher return rate from the group account.
  • If you have a larger portion of the total endowments, you'll receive a lower return rate from the group account.
